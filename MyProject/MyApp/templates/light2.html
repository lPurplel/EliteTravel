Overview Summary
Launched in 2009 by Brazilian manufacturer Embraer as a larger version of its successful Phenom 100, the Phenom 300 has since become a firm favourite in the private jet charter market.
Comfort & Size
The Phenom 300/300E features a BMW-designed interior, which is both visually and ergonomically appealing. It also features the only seats of any aircraft in the light jet category that can be converted to lie-flat beds. The aircraft comfortably seats up to 7, with a refreshment area and aft lavatory.
